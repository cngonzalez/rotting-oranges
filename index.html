Rotting Oranges

A visual representation of a breadth-first implementation.

Imagine you have a box of oranges. Every minute, any rotten orange in the box will also rot its immediate neighbors. How long will it take to rot the entire box of oranges?

Instructions:

  1. If you like, change the grid dimensions with the given input boxes (try to keep it reasonable!)
  2. Click each box of the grid to fill it with a fresh orange, rotting orange, or nothing.
  3. Click "Start timer" to see what happens!
